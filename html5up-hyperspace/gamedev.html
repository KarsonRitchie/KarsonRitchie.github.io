Me as a Game Developer

As a hobby, I love to make games. I develop games in the Godot Game Engine, and with the help of the internet and my friends to provide me assets like art and music. With my friends and the internet providing the assets, my work is mostly in game design and programming.

Most of my portfolio is demonstrations or prototypes since I have failed to produce a full game as of yet. However, I am working on one currently. Most of my work though, including the project is in the survival horror genre, with that being my favorite genre of video games.
